<p style="font-size:26px; font-weight:bold; color:purple;">Learnings:</p>
<ul>
<li>Pseudo-elements: Style specific parts of an element, like adding something before or after it.</li>

<li>Pseudo-classes: Change how an element looks in certain states, like when you hover or click it.</li>

<li>Visibility: Makes an element visible or hidden.</li>

<li>Helps make websites interactive and neat without adding extra elements.</li>
