Memories

Intrams

Our school sports event where everyone competed and had fun.



Litrato mula kay Nicole at sa Paham

Although I did not participate in Intrams, I learned about the value of sportsmanship and staying active. Watching my classmates compete made me realize how important teamwork and discipline are, both in sports and in life. I would explain to others that Intrams is a time to encourage physical fitness and cooperation. Having this event motivates students to stay healthy and work well with others.

AP Month

Celebrating Araling Panlipunan with projects, performances, and exhibits.


Litrato mula sa Paham

In AP Month, I participated in the quiz held inside the classroom. This helped me review and strengthen my knowledge of Araling Panlipunan topics. I realized that learning about history and government is important because it helps us understand real-life situations and current events better. If I were to teach a friend, I would tell them that AP Month makes learning fun and interactive through quizzes and projects. Events like this help us remember important lessons and apply them outside school.

Science Month

We had experiments, quizzes, and fun science activities.


Litrato mula kay Tamara at sa Paham

During Science Month, I participated in the research defense with my group. This experience taught me how to prepare, present, and defend our research, which improved my confidence and scientific understanding. I would explain Science Month as a time to explore experiments and research projects that develop critical thinking. This event is important because it helps us build skills that can solve real-world problems and improve our future.

Buwan ng Wika

A celebration of Filipino language and culture through speeches and performances.


Litrato mula sa Paham

During Buwan ng Wika, I learned the importance of celebrating our Filipino culture and traditions. I was surprised and happy to be chosen for the best attire in our level, which made me appreciate our heritage even more. This event showed me how important it is to respect and promote our culture in everyday life. If I were to explain it to a classmate, I would say it’s a special celebration of Filipino language and history through fun activities.

Having this event helps us build pride in who we are as Filipinos.

Teachers Day

We visited museums and learned about history and science outside the classroom.


Litrato mula sa Paham at sa Faith

For Teachers’ Day, I actively joined the choir for our music video and helped with the intermission and planning of the program. Through this event, I learned about teamwork and the joy of honoring our teachers for their hard work. Teaching this to a friend, I’d say it’s a special day when we show gratitude by organizing fun activities. It’s important because it motivates teachers and strengthens the student-teacher relationship.
